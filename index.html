My name is Haoyang Fu, and I am currently a Research Fellow at the School of Materials Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with A/P Shuzhou Li. My research interests focus on applying AI to develop advanced catalytic materials for efficient green catalytic transformation and sustainable valorization of emerging pollutants. I received my Ph.D. in Environmental Science from Tongji University and was previously a visiting scholar in Computational Science at NTU. Before that, I obtained my M.E. degree from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and B.E. degree from Fuzhou University.

Research Interests

  • AI-assisted discovery of catalysts for enhanced Fenton catalysis
  • Structural engineering of nanomaterials for catalytic upvaluing of emerging pollutants
  • Investigation of particle growth mechanisms and shape anisotropy using Cs-TEM and liquid-cell TEM
